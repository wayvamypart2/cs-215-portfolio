## Final Project Ideas

### Idea 1: Global Literacy Rates Over Time
- How have global literacy rates changed over the last 50 years?
- Which regions have improved the most/least in literacy rates?
- Are there countries that show stagnation or decline in literacy trends?

### Idea 2: Literacy vs Economic Development
- Is there a correlation between literacy rates and GDP per capita?
- Do countries with higher literacy rates tend to have higher human development index (HDI)?
- Which countries are outliers in this relationship?

### Idea 3: Gender Gap in Literacy
- How large is the gender gap in literacy rates across countries?
- Which regions have the largest improvements in female literacy over time?
- Is the gender literacy gap shrinking globally?
